About

The sound between
silence and static

Maya Chen grew up between Taipei and Vancouver, finding music in the hum of fluorescent lights and the rhythm of rainfall on steel rooftops. After a decade of classical training, she dismantled everything she knew to build something entirely new.

Her work has been featured in Pitchfork, The FADER, and Resident Advisor, and she's collaborated with artists across electronic, ambient, and experimental pop.
