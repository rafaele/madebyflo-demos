FAQ

Common questions

How does the free trial work?

Start with a 14-day free trial of Pro — no credit card required. You get full access to all features. At the end, choose a plan or stay on Starter for free.

Can I change plans later?

Absolutely. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime from your billing settings. Changes take effect immediately with prorated billing.

What integrations do you support?

We integrate with 200+ tools including Slack, GitHub, Jira, Linear, Notion, and all major cloud providers. Plus our API lets you build custom integrations.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We're SOC 2 Type II certified with end-to-end encryption. Data is stored in your preferred region with full GDPR compliance.
