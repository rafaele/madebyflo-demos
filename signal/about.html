About the Writer

Alex Morgan

I'm a designer, writer, and perpetual student of how things work. For the past decade, I've worked at the intersection of design and technology — first at agencies, then in-house at startups, and now independently.

Signal started in 2022 as a way to think out loud. What began as notes to a few friends has grown into a weekly practice that reaches thousands of curious minds.

I believe the best ideas live at the edges — between disciplines, between cultures, between the obvious and the overlooked. That's what I try to find every week.

The Mission

Why Signal exists

There's no shortage of information in the world. What's scarce is thoughtful interpretation — someone willing to sit with an idea long enough to find what's actually interesting about it.

Signal is my attempt to be that filter. Each week, I pick one idea and explore it deeply: where it came from, why it matters, and what it might mean for how we design, build, and create.

I write for designers, developers, founders, and anyone who cares about making things with intention. If you've ever felt overwhelmed by the pace of change but still want to stay curious, this newsletter is for you.
