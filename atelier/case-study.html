The Challenge

Lumière Parfums approached us with a bold ambition: to launch a niche fragrance house that would challenge the conventions of luxury perfumery. They needed a brand identity that felt both timeless and distinctly modern.

The Process

We began with an immersive discovery phase, spending time in the founder's atelier, understanding the craft of perfume-making and the stories behind each composition.

The visual identity draws from the interplay of light and shadow — a nod to the brand name. The logomark uses a custom-drawn serif that feels hand-crafted without being precious.

The Result

The brand launched to critical acclaim. Within six months, Lumière was stocked in 40 boutiques across Europe and had developed a devoted following among collectors. The packaging design was featured in Print Magazine's Annual Design Awards.
